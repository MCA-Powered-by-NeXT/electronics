How To Use This Reader

Use the weekly reader files before, during, or after class. The daily lesson pages tell students what to do in class. The textbook reader files explain the concepts behind those activities in longer textbook-style sections.

Each weekly reader includes five daily readings. Each daily reading links directly to the matching lesson page, and each reading includes 5-10 paragraphs of explanation, common beginner mistakes, technician habits, and course-specific safety reminders.

Course safety boundary: These readings use assigned low-voltage classroom examples. Students do not work on wall power, unknown energized equipment, open power supplies, vehicle electrical systems, charged capacitors, or non-training circuits.
