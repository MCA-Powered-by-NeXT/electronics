How To Use This Page

Students no longer need to open a separate reader before class. Each daily lesson now begins with a Student Reading: Learn This First section followed by the day's lesson work, lab task, checkoff, quiz, or exit ticket.

Older weekly reader files may remain in the repository as background/reference material, but the daily lesson pages are now the primary student-facing source.
