Week 1 Student Quizzes

Ten questions for each daily lesson. Write complete answers in your notebook or on the assigned quiz sheet.

Day 1 Quiz: Safety and Technician Habits

  1. What is the purpose of the technician safety loop?
  2. Name three bench conditions that should be corrected before lab work begins.
  3. What does PPE mean, and why does it matter?
  4. Why should a student stop when the power state is unclear?
  5. What is one electrical hazard a beginner should recognize?
  6. What is the difference between working carefully and guessing?
  7. Why is documentation part of safe work?
  8. What should you do before changing circuit wiring?
  9. How does a clear bench help prevent mistakes?
  10. Write one technician habit you will practice this week.

Day 2 Quiz: Tinkercad Circuits

  1. What is a circuit simulation?
  2. Why do we use Tinkercad before some physical builds?
  3. Name four parts used in the first simulated LED circuit.
  4. What does Start Simulation do?
  5. What does it mean to predict before testing?
  6. What should happen when a switch opens a circuit path?
  7. Why should the LED have a resistor in the circuit?
  8. What observation would you record if the simulated LED does not turn on?
  9. How can simulation help you avoid unsafe habits?
  10. Describe the simulated circuit path from source to return.

Day 3 Quiz: Electricity and Circuit Model

  1. Define source, path, load, and return.
  2. What is a complete circuit path?
  3. What is an open circuit condition?
  4. What is an unintended path condition?
  5. Why does current need a complete path?
  6. What does an insulator do?
  7. What does a conductor do?
  8. How can a switch control a circuit?
  9. Why is the return path important?
  10. Explain a simple LED circuit using the four-part circuit model.

Day 4 Quiz: Lab 1 Safety Inspection

  1. What is the purpose of a safety inspection?
  2. Name three items to inspect before beginning lab work.
  3. Why should damaged leads or tools be reported?
  4. What should you do if a wire or part looks unsafe?
  5. Why do students inspect before powering a circuit?
  6. What is one PPE item used in this course?
  7. Why should liquids stay away from lab benches?
  8. What is a safe response to an unclear instruction?
  9. What should be included in a lab signoff note?
  10. Write one safety correction you might make during bench setup.

Day 5 Quiz: Week 1 Review and Readiness

  1. What is the most important safety rule from Week 1?
  2. How does Tinkercad support physical lab work?
  3. What are the four parts of the circuit model?
  4. What is one difference between an open path and a complete path?
  5. Why should students disconnect power before changing wiring?
  6. What does a technician document after a lab activity?
  7. Name one electrical hazard and one safe response.
  8. What does it mean to work inside the classroom lab boundary?
  9. What skill from Week 1 will help in Week 2?
  10. Write one question you still have before moving to Week 2.
