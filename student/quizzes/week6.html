Week 6 Student Quizzes

Ten questions for each daily lesson.

Quiz PacketsStudent HubWeek 6

Day 1 Quiz: Final Project Planning

  1. What is the purpose of the final project plan?
  2. Why must the project be instructor approved?
  3. Name four items that belong in a parts list.
  4. What should the test plan explain?
  5. Why should source, path, load, and return be identified before building?
  6. What is a control point?
  7. What safety checks belong in the plan?
  8. Why should project scope not change without approval?
  9. What evidence will prove your project works?
  10. Write one planning question you need answered before building.

Day 2 Quiz: Build and Initial Test

  1. Why should parts be checked against the approved plan?
  2. What should be inspected before first power-up?
  3. Why does clean wiring matter?
  4. What source information should be recorded?
  5. What is an initial function test?
  6. Why should measurements include units?
  7. What should happen if the project does not work at first?
  8. Why should wiring changes be documented?
  9. What is one common final project build mistake?
  10. Write a project status note using evidence.

Day 3 Quiz: Troubleshoot, Revise, Document, and Maintenance

  1. What is the first step when a final project has a problem?
  2. Why should the original symptom be recorded?
  3. What evidence can support a correction?
  4. Why should only one change be made at a time?
  5. What does final verification show?
  6. What belongs in the final documentation packet?
  7. What is preventive maintenance?
  8. Name one maintenance check for training equipment.
  9. How does documentation support maintenance?
  10. Write one maintenance observation for your project or station.

Day 4 Quiz: Final Demonstration

  1. What is the purpose of the final demonstration?
  2. What should you explain first during your demo?
  3. How should you identify source, path, load, and return?
  4. What measurement or test evidence can you show?
  5. Why should safety habits be part of the demonstration?
  6. What should your documentation prove?
  7. How should you respond to instructor questions?
  8. What is one improvement you could make to your project?
  9. Why is communication a technician skill?
  10. Write a short demonstration script for your project.

Day 5 Quiz: Final Quiz, Certificate, and Workplace Expectations

  1. What safety habit will you use after this course?
  2. Define source, path, load, and return.
  3. What DMM setup habit is most important?
  4. Why is continuity testing done with power off?
  5. What does troubleshooting with evidence mean?
  6. What belongs in a professional technician note?
  7. Name three workplace expectations for technicians.
  8. How does tool care affect reliability?
  9. What career pathway interests you most?
  10. What technician skill will you practice next?
