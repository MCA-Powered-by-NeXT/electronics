Safety-Critical Criteria

Automatic retry: Student must verify power is disconnected before continuity testing. Testing continuity on an energized or unknown circuit requires stopping and reteaching.

Scoring Guidance

Pass requires all safety-critical criteria and correct interpretation of at least wire, switch, and fuse tests.
