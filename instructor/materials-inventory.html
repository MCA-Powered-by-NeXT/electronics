Instructor Only

Adjust quantities based on class size. A useful planning unit is one kit per two students plus instructor demonstration spares.

ItemSuggested QuantityNotes

Components and Consumables

  • LEDs in several colors
  • Resistors: 220 ohm, 330 ohm, 470 ohm, 1 k ohm, 10 k ohm
  • Momentary and toggle switches
  • Training fuses and fuse holders
  • Jumper wires and wire samples
  • Simple buzzers or lamps for final project options
  • Connectors and terminals for inspection practice
  • Printed lab sheets, quiz pages, checkoff forms, and repair tickets

Reset and Maintenance Notes

  • Sort damaged LEDs, opened fuses, and suspect wires into a clearly marked instructor-only bin.
  • Check batteries before labs that rely on visible LED brightness.
  • Keep answer keys separate from student quiz pages.
  • Keep fault-board fault maps private.
