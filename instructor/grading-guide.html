Instructor Only

Adapt grading weights to local program rules while preserving safety-critical pass/retry expectations. The current course is built around daily lesson participation, weekly quiz packets, lab documentation, practical checkoffs, and a final project packet/demonstration.

Suggested Weighting

CategorySuggested WeightEvidence

Labs, worksheets, and textbook-reader responses25-30%Inspection records, build tables, measurement tables, schematic annotations, repair tickets.
Practical checkoffs20-25%DMM checkoff, continuity/tracing checkoff, troubleshooting checkoff.
Final project20-25%Plan, build/test record, troubleshooting documentation, demonstration rubric, certificate checklist.

Safety-Critical Pass/Retry Items

  • Student works only on assigned low-voltage training circuits and sources.
  • Student disconnects power before rewiring, resistance testing, continuity testing, or changing components.
  • Student does not use current mode independently.
  • Student controls DMM probes and checks jacks before measurement.
  • Student does not bypass protective components or instructor safety boundaries.
  • Student stops and asks when source, circuit state, or procedure is unclear.

Retake and Retry Guidance

Allow quiz corrections for learning. Require practical retry for unsafe DMM setup, powered continuity testing, bypassing protective devices, energizing a circuit before inspection, or troubleshooting without evidence. Retries should target the missed habit rather than repeating the entire week.
