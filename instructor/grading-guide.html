Suggested Weighting

CategoryWeightNotes
Weekly quizzes30%Use for vocabulary, concept checks, calculations, and interpretation.
Labs and worksheets30%Require completion, safe behavior, and measurement records.
Practical checkoffs20%DMM, continuity, and troubleshooting.
Final project20%Use final project rubric and certificate checklist.

Competency-Based Completion

  • Students must complete all required labs or approved make-up labs.
  • Students must pass safety-critical checkoffs.
  • Unsafe actions result in immediate coaching and retry, not silent point deduction only.
  • Documentation should be evaluated for clarity, units, expected/actual results, and final verification.

Retake and Retry Guidance

Allow quiz corrections for learning. Require practical retry for unsafe DMM setup, powered continuity testing, bypassing protective devices, or energizing a circuit before inspection.
