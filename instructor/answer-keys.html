Quiz Page Cleanup Decision

The course now uses a clear separation:

  • Student quiz pages: one student-facing index plus six weekly quiz packets. These pages contain prompts only.
  • Instructor quiz answers: one canonical answer-key page, Daily Quiz Answer Keys.
  • This page: assessment hub and workflow guide, not a second copy of the answers.

Instructor Scoring Guidance

  • Accept equivalent wording when the safety habit and technical concept are correct.
  • Do not grade only for memorized phrasing. Grade for correct reasoning, vocabulary, units, circuit state, and evidence.
  • Safety-critical errors require coaching and retry even when a written answer is partly correct.
  • For labs and checkoffs, evidence should include what was observed, what was expected, what was tested, what changed, and how the result was verified.
  • For final project completion, require safe work habits, circuit explanation, documentation, and demonstration evidence.
