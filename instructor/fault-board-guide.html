Instructor Only

Use only low-voltage DC trainer boards. Do not plant faults involving wall power, high voltage, charged capacitors, vehicle systems, or unknown equipment.

Recommended Training Board

A simple fault board can include a battery holder, fuse holder or training fuse, switch, current-limiting resistor, LED or buzzer load, and labeled test points.

BT1 + -> F1 -> SW1 -> R1 -> LED1 -> BT1 -
Test points: TP1 source, TP2 after fuse, TP3 after switch, TP4 load return
