Instructor Use

Use this hub for planning and scoring. Student-facing links are included for projection and navigation, but answer-key pages should not be shared directly with students. The course now uses daily 2-3 hour lesson pages, a student textbook reader, weekly student quiz packets, and instructor-only answer keys/checkoff guidance.

Primary Instructor Workflow

  1. Open the Pacing Guide for the day and confirm the 2-3 hour delivery plan.
  2. Use the daily week page and the Textbook Reader for student explanations and conceptual review.
  3. Prepare stations with the Lab Setup Guide and Materials Inventory.
  4. Use the Student Quiz Packets for student prompts.
  5. Use Daily Quiz Answer Keys as the canonical answer-key source and Assessment Hub for scoring workflow.
