Instructor Only

Use this page to prepare benches, tools, parts, and safety controls before students arrive. All labs must remain within the low-voltage DC training boundary. Do not create real hazards for demonstration.

General Safety Setup

  • Use only low-voltage battery-powered DC training circuits and approved classroom trainers.
  • Remove access to wall outlets as measurement points.
  • Inspect DMM leads and remove damaged leads from service.
  • Use current-limiting resistors for all LED circuits.
  • Keep damaged LEDs, opened fuses, suspect wires, and planted-fault materials in instructor-only bins.
  • Keep answer keys separate from student quiz packets.
  • Label final project kits and fault boards clearly.

Daily Bench Checklist

  • [ ] Safety glasses available.
  • [ ] DMM leads inspected.
  • [ ] Red DMM leads are not left in current jacks unless used in instructor-controlled demo.
  • [ ] No unsafe high-energy equipment accessible.
  • [ ] Batteries/training sources checked.
  • [ ] Component bins labeled.
  • [ ] Student pages, quiz packets, or checkoff forms ready.
  • [ ] Cleanup containers ready.
  • [ ] Instructor-only props clearly separated from student-use parts.
  • [ ] Answer keys not visible on projected/student screens.

Instructor Projection Guidance

Project student-facing daily pages, the Student Hub, or the Textbook Reader. Do not project instructor answer keys, fault maps, private reset notes, or grading records while students are present.
