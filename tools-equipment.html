Tools and Equipment

Required equipment, parts, consumables, and safety materials for the 6-week course.

DashboardSyllabusCourse MapCompetencies

Student and Lab Tools

  • Digital multimeter with working leads and internal fuse protection
  • Safety glasses
  • Wire strippers and small screwdrivers
  • Breadboards or electrical training boards
  • Low-voltage batteries and instructor-approved DC power supplies
  • Assorted jumper wires, solid and stranded wire samples
  • LEDs, resistors, switches, fuses, fuse holders, simple buzzers or lamps
  • Connectors and terminals for inspection and continuity practice

Consumables and Preparation

  • Resistors in common values such as 220 ohm, 330 ohm, 470 ohm, 1 k ohm, 10 k ohm
  • Spare LEDs and fuses for intentional failure examples
  • Printed lab sheets, quizzes, and checkoff forms
  • Fault boards with removable or resettable faults

Safety Boundary

Instructor note: Do not use wall outlets, open power supplies, vehicle electrical systems, charged capacitors, or unknown energized equipment for beginner labs. Use low-voltage training circuits only.
