Student Checkoff Overview

  • [ ] I can explain and follow lab safety rules.
  • [ ] I can identify voltage, current, resistance, continuity, and classroom source types.
  • [ ] I can identify approved low-voltage classroom sources and record source labels.
  • [ ] I can use a DMM for approved classroom measurements.
  • [ ] I can explain special-use meter-setting stop rules.
  • [ ] I can perform power-off continuity tests.
  • [ ] I can identify wires, connectors, terminals, and common components.
  • [ ] I can read a simple schematic and build a matching circuit.
  • [ ] I can troubleshoot a simple problem and verify the repair.
  • [ ] I can complete a final project with documentation.
  • [ ] I can describe preventive maintenance and workplace expectations.
