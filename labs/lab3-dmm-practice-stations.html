Lab 3: DMM Practice Stations

Week 3 required lab. Rotate through safe low-voltage measurement stations.

Week 3Lesson 5Lesson 6Week 3 QuizDMM Checkoff

Safety Warning

Meter setup matters: Verify lead jacks and dial setting before every station. Do not use current mode unless an instructor directly approves the specific setup.

Purpose and Skills Practiced

  • Set up a DMM for DC voltage, resistance, and continuity checks.
  • Read meter units correctly.
  • Record measurements in a consistent log.
  • Develop a pre-measurement checklist habit.

Tools and Parts

  • DMM and leads
  • Known battery or low-voltage DC source
  • Resistor samples
  • Disconnected wire samples
  • Station cards

Setup Diagram

Station A: DC battery voltage
Station B: resistor value with power off
Station C: wire continuity with power off
Station D: polarity identification

Procedure

  1. At each station, read the station card before touching the meter.
  2. Write the measurement type: voltage, resistance, or continuity.
  3. Set the leads and dial correctly.
  4. Ask the instructor for approval at the first station.
  5. Measure and record the value with units.
  6. Reset the station for the next group.

Cleanup and Reset

  1. Return all samples to their station cards.
  2. Turn off or reset the meter.
  3. Coil leads neatly.
  4. Report damaged leads or unclear station cards.
