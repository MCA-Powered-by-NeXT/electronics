Lab 6: Series and Parallel Circuits

Week 4 required lab. Build, measure, and compare circuit paths.

Week 4Lesson 9Week 4 QuizContinuity Checkoff

Safety Warning

Inspect before power: Check polarity and confirm no direct positive-to-negative short before connecting the battery.

Purpose and Skills Practiced

  • Build series and parallel LED circuits.
  • Measure low-voltage DC circuit points.
  • Compare fault behavior when one load is removed.
  • Document observations using correct units.

Tools and Parts

  • DMM
  • Battery or approved low-voltage DC supply
  • Breadboard
  • LEDs
  • Current-limiting resistors
  • Jumper wires

Setup Diagram

Series test:
+ -> R1 -> LED1 -> LED2 -> -

Parallel test:
+ -> R1 -> LED1 -> -
+ -> R2 -> LED2 -> -

Procedure

  1. Build the series circuit with power disconnected.
  2. Inspect wiring and LED polarity.
  3. Connect low-voltage power and observe brightness.
  4. Measure source voltage and selected voltage drops as instructed.
  5. Disconnect power and remove one LED. Reconnect and observe.
  6. Repeat the process with the parallel circuit.
  7. Compare results and explain the difference.
