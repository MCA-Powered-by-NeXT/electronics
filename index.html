Course Overview

This site is organized as a Basic Electronics Technician workforce course. The sequence builds from safety and electrical foundations to DMM use, continuity testing, schematics, circuit building, troubleshooting, documentation, and final project demonstration.

Safety boundary: Student labs use assigned low-voltage classroom training circuits only. Students do not work on non-training building power, open power supplies, vehicle electrical systems, or unknown energized equipment.
