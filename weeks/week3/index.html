Week 3: Digital Multimeter and Continuity Testing

Five daily class meetings, 2-3 hours each. Students practice correct DMM setup, approved low-voltage measurements, resistance readings, and power-off continuity testing.

Learning Outcomes

  • Identify DMM parts, jacks, leads, and common symbols.
  • Select correct settings for DC voltage, resistance, and continuity.
  • Record measurements with units and notes.
  • Perform continuity tests only on circuits verified power-off.

Instructor Notes

Use only low-voltage batteries and unpowered components. Include intentionally wrong meter setups as discussion props, not student tasks.
