Week 3: Digital Multimeter and Continuity Testing

Five daily class meetings, 2-3 hours each. Students learn meter setup, approved low-voltage measurements, power-off continuity testing, and DMM practical checkoff skills.

Week Three Purpose

Week Three teaches students how to use a digital multimeter as a technician tool. The emphasis is on setup, circuit state, correct jacks, correct settings, controlled probes, units, and documentation.

DMM safety note: Students use approved low-voltage training circuits only. Continuity and resistance are measured only with power disconnected.
