Week 1 Day 1: Safety and Technician Habits

2-3 hour class block focused on course orientation, safety mindset, technician habits, and hazard recognition.

Week 1Next DayLesson 1Lab 1

Learning Targets

  • Describe what entry-level electronics technicians do.
  • Use the technician safety loop: inspect, identify hazards, control power, verify, work, test, document.
  • Identify common lab hazards before touching tools or circuits.

Student Task

Complete Lesson 1. For the exit ticket, write one sentence explaining why a technician inspects before touching a circuit.
