Week 1 Day 2: Tinkercad Circuits

2-3 hour class block dedicated to Tinkercad circuit simulation before physical circuit work.

Week 1Previous DayNext DayTinkercad Lesson

Learning Targets

  • Open and navigate Tinkercad Circuits.
  • Place a battery, switch, resistor, LED, and wires.
  • Use build, predict, simulate, explain, and document as the circuit workflow.

Class Agenda

TimeActivityEvidence
20-30 minInstructor demonstration: workspace, parts, wiring, start/stop simulationStudents identify the workspace and component panel.
30-45 minGuided build: simple LED circuitWorking simulated circuit or annotated instructor demo.
25-35 minPrediction practice: open switch, missing return, reversed LEDPrediction table completed.
30-45 minStudent mini-assignment and documentationTechnician note with source, path, load, return.
10-20 minShare-out and troubleshooting discussionStudent explains one mistake and correction.

Tinkercad Access

Use Tinkercad Circuits. Follow your instructor's sign-in directions. If individual student accounts are not available, complete the activity as an instructor-led projected demonstration with written student responses.

Student Task

Complete Making Circuits in Tinkercad. Save, screenshot, sketch, or describe your simulated circuit as directed by the instructor.
