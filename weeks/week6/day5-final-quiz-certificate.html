Day 5 Purpose

Students complete final assessment items, verify required competencies, submit final documentation, and connect course skills to technician pathways. This day closes the course with evidence of what students can do, not just what they have heard.

Learning Targets

  • Complete the final quiz.
  • Review required course competencies.
  • Submit final project documentation.
  • Identify possible next-step career or training pathways.
  • Reflect on the strongest technician habit developed during the course.

Final Quiz

  1. Explain the technician safety loop.
  2. Define source, path, load, and return.
  3. Explain the difference between open and short circuits.
  4. Name the correct safety habit before rewiring.
  5. Explain what a resistor does in an LED circuit.
  6. State one DMM setup check before measuring voltage.
  7. Explain why continuity testing is done with power off.
  8. Describe a systematic troubleshooting sequence.
  9. Explain what belongs in a repair ticket.
  10. Describe how your final project was verified.
  11. Write one example of a professional technician note.
  12. Name one career area where these skills could be useful.

Certificate Competency Checklist

  • [ ] Demonstrates safe low-voltage lab habits.
  • [ ] Identifies basic components and symbols.
  • [ ] Builds a simple DC training circuit.
  • [ ] Uses DMM setup checks before measurement.
  • [ ] Performs continuity testing with power off.
  • [ ] Troubleshoots using evidence.
  • [ ] Documents measurements, faults, corrections, and verification.
  • [ ] Completes final project demonstration.
  • [ ] Explains next-step technician skill goals.

Final Submission Checklist
