Week 6: Final Project, Documentation, and Career Pathways

Five daily class meetings, 2-3 hours each. Students plan, build, test, troubleshoot, document, present, and complete final certificate checkoffs.

Week Six Purpose

Week Six is performance-based. Students demonstrate safe work habits, circuit understanding, DMM use, continuity reasoning, troubleshooting, documentation, and professional communication through a final low-voltage project.

Safety boundary: Final projects must be approved low-voltage DC projects. Students disconnect power before rewiring and request instructor approval before changing project scope.
