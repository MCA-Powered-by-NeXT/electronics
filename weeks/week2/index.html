Week 2: Voltage, Current, Resistance, and Basic Circuits

Students use simple numbers and low-voltage LED circuits to understand Ohm's Law, current limiting, batteries, polarity, and AC versus DC at an introductory level.

Module Description

This week turns vocabulary into technician decisions. Students learn how voltage pushes current through resistance, why LEDs need current-limiting resistors, and how to check battery polarity and voltage before building.

Learning Outcomes

  • Define voltage, current, resistance, and power in plain language.
  • Use Ohm's Law for simple calculations with volts, amps, and ohms.
  • Decode common 4-band resistor color codes.
  • Build and test a simple battery-powered LED circuit safely.

Required Lessons and Lab

  • Lesson 3: Voltage, Current, and Resistance
  • Lesson 4: Ohm's Law, AC/DC, Batteries, and Power Supplies
  • Lab 2: Battery Voltage and Simple LED Circuit
