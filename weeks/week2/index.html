Week 2: Voltage, Current, Resistance, and Basic Circuits

Five daily class meetings, 2-3 hours each. Students use simple numbers and low-voltage LED circuits to understand voltage, current, resistance, Ohm's Law, polarity, and current limiting.

Learning Outcomes

  • Define voltage, current, resistance, and power in plain language.
  • Use Ohm's Law for simple calculations with volts, amps, and ohms.
  • Decode common 4-band resistor color codes.
  • Build and test a simple battery-powered LED circuit safely.

Instructor Notes

Prepare batteries, resistors, LEDs, and enough spare LEDs for polarity mistakes. Keep supply voltages low and require students to inspect before powering circuits.
