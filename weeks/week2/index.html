Week Two Purpose

Week Two turns the Week 1 circuit model into simple electrical quantities. Students learn voltage, current, resistance, source identification, approved classroom DC sources, resistor values, and introductory Ohm's Law.

Safety boundary: Student labs use low-voltage battery-powered DC circuits or instructor-approved classroom DC sources only.
