Week 4 Day 2: Schematic Symbols and Circuit Tracing

2-3 hour class block connecting physical parts to schematic symbols and source-to-return tracing.

Week 4Previous DayNext Day

Day 2 Purpose

Students learn that schematics are simplified maps of circuit connections. They practice reading symbols and tracing the intended path from source through load and back to return.

Learning Targets

  • Recognize common schematic symbols.
  • Identify reference labels such as R1, LED1, SW1, and B1.
  • Trace a complete path on a simple schematic.
  • Compare schematic layout to physical layout.
