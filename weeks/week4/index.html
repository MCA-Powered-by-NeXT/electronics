Week 4: Components, Wiring, Schematics, Series and Parallel

Five daily class meetings, 2-3 hours each. Students connect physical components to schematic symbols, trace circuits, and compare series and parallel behavior.

Week Four Purpose

Week Four helps students move between the physical circuit on a bench and the schematic drawing technicians use to plan, build, inspect, and troubleshoot.

Safety boundary: All builds remain low-voltage DC training circuits. Students disconnect power before changing components or wiring.
