Week 4: Components, Wiring, Schematics, Series and Parallel

Five daily class meetings, 2-3 hours each. Students identify common parts, read simple schematics, trace nodes, and compare series and parallel circuit behavior.

Learning Outcomes

  • Identify resistors, LEDs, diodes, capacitors, switches, fuses, wires, connectors, and terminals.
  • Recognize common schematic symbols and reference labels.
  • Trace a simple circuit from source to load to return.
  • Explain how voltage and current behave differently in series and parallel circuits.

Instructor Notes

Prepare mixed component trays, schematic cards, and simple build stations with spare resistors and LEDs.
